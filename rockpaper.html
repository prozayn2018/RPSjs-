This rock-paper-scissors game illustrates the basic principles of an adaptive artificial intelligence technology. The system learns to identify patterns of a person’s behavior by analysing their decision strategies in order to predict future behavior. While a computer won’t win all rounds, over time it can learn a person’s behavioral characteristics to perform better than chance.
